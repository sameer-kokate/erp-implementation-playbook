# ERP Implementation Playbook

A practical guide for executing successful ERP implementations in discrete manufacturing environments. This playbook is based on real-world project experience across multiple full lifecycle ERP deployments.

---

## 🔹 1. Requirement Gathering

- Conduct stakeholder interviews (Production, Procurement, Finance)
- Understand current system limitations
- Capture business requirements and pain points

---

## 🔹 2. Fit-Gap Analysis

- Map business requirements to ERP capabilities
- Identify gaps and define solutions
- Prioritize gaps based on business impact

---

## 🔹 3. Business Process Reengineering (BPR)

- Document As-Is processes
- Design To-Be processes aligned with ERP
- Eliminate manual and redundant steps

---

## 🔹 4. Solution Design

- Prepare Functional Specification Documents (FSD)
- Define workflows, data structures, and business rules
- Align with stakeholders before development

---

## 🔹 5. Configuration & Development

- Configure ERP modules (MRP, Production, Procurement)
- Collaborate with technical teams for custom solutions
- Validate configurations against business scenarios

---

## 🔹 6. User Acceptance Testing (UAT)

- Prepare test scenarios and scripts
- Train key users
- Capture feedback and resolve issues

---

## 🔹 7. Go-Live Preparation

- Final data migration
- End-user training
- Cutover planning and risk mitigation

---

## 🔹 8. Go-Live & Hypercare

- Monitor system performance
- Resolve critical issues
- Support users during transition

---

## 🔹 9. Continuous Improvement

- Identify enhancement opportunities
- Optimize processes post go-live
- Introduce automation and AI-driven improvements

---

## 🚀 Key Focus Areas

- MRP Optimization
- Production Planning
- Shopfloor Execution
- Inventory Management
- Compliance (GST, SST, e-Invoicing)
